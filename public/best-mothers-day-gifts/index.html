When is Mother's Day ?

Mother's Day falls on the second Sunday of May each year — in that's May 11th in the US, Canada, and Australia. Order early, especially for custom or shipped items.

What are the best last-minute Mother's Day gifts?

Luxury candles, spa gift cards, premium skincare sets, and flower subscriptions can all be ordered quickly and still feel intentional. Add a handwritten note and even a simple gift becomes memorable.

What do moms really want for Mother's Day?

Most moms want to feel truly seen — a gift tied to her specific interests, a comfort she'd never buy herself, or an experience that gives her time to breathe. Specificity always beats generosity.

How much should I spend on a Mother's Day gift?

Thoughtfulness consistently outperforms price. A $45 candle she adores beats a $200 item she doesn't know what to do with. Focus on resonance, not the receipt.
