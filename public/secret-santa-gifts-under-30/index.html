Under $30 FAQ

Getting the most out of the sweet spot budget.

Is $30 a good Secret Santa budget?

$25–$30 is actually the sweet spot for Secret Santa — enough to get something genuinely nice without overspending or making others feel awkward. Most groups land in this range for good reason.

What are the best Secret Santa gifts around $30?

At the $30 mark you can get things that really feel premium — artisan food sets, fun games, quality candles, or a nice drinkware set. You have just enough budget to add real quality and personality.

What Secret Santa gifts work for people you don't know well?

Stick to consumables and universally loved categories — food, games, candles, or plants. Avoid anything too personal, too niche, or requiring taste knowledge you don't have. Food and games are the safest bets at this price point.
