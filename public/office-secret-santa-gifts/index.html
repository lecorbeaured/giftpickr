Office Exchange FAQ

Navigating workplace gift-giving without incident.

What are good office Secret Santa gifts?

Good office picks are professional, universally appealing, and safe for all workplace settings. Top choices include premium food items, quality desk accessories, nice candles, fun tasteful card games, and cozy comfort items like mugs or quality socks.

What should you avoid in an office Secret Santa?

Avoid anything too personal, political, religious, or edgy. Skip alcohol unless you know the recipient well, anything with very strong scents, and anything that could be perceived as inappropriate. The goal is to delight, not create an HR situation.

What is a good office Secret Santa budget?

Most office exchanges cap at $20–$30. Under $25 is the most common limit. The goal is a thoughtful, workplace-appropriate gift that feels genuine — not to impress or overspend relative to colleagues.
