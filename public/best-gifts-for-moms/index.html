Gifting Questions, Answered

What's the best last-minute gift for mom?

A luxury candle, a digital spa gift card, or a beautifully packaged tea set can be ordered quickly and still feel personal and elevated. Add a handwritten card to make it feel intentional.

What do moms actually want as gifts?

Most moms appreciate gifts that signal someone was paying attention — something tied to her interests, her routines, or a comfort she'd never buy herself. Practical beauty, quiet luxury, and experiences tend to land best.

What's a good gift for a mom who has everything?

Experiences and consumables work beautifully — a spa afternoon, a wine subscription, premium teas or skincare. Things she uses up and enjoys in the moment, rather than things that sit on a shelf.

How much should I spend on a gift for mom?

There's no rule, but thoughtfulness consistently outweighs price. A $40 candle she adores beats a $200 item she doesn't know what to do with. Focus on resonance over cost.
