What are the best Valentine's Day gifts for him?

Top picks include quality whiskey or craft beer sets, premium grooming kits, tech gadgets, personalized keepsakes, experience gifts like a sports event or cooking class, and specialty food and drink. The best gifts are always tied to something he actually does.

What do men want for Valentine's Day?

Most men appreciate practical gifts they'd use daily, premium versions of things they already love, or experiences tied to something they're passionate about. Skip the generic chocolate box — something specific to him will always land better.

Is Valentine's Day a big deal for guys?

It varies — but most men appreciate being thought of and given something that shows their partner knows them. A well-chosen gift tied to his actual interests lands far better than something grabbed from a Valentine's display.
