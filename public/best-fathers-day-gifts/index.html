Father's Day Questions

Everything you need to nail the gift this year.

When is Father's Day ?

Father's Day falls on the third Sunday of June in the United States, Canada, and the United Kingdom. Order early — especially for personalized or shipped items — to guarantee delivery in time.

What are the most popular Father's Day gifts?

Top picks year after year include grilling gear, tech gadgets, personalized keepsakes, experiences like sports tickets or golf outings, premium grooming kits, and subscriptions to things he loves — coffee, whiskey, streaming.

What should I get a dad who says he doesn't want anything?

Get him something consumable and premium — great whiskey, specialty coffee, a steak sampler, or a quality grooming set. He'll use it, enjoy it, and never feel obligated to keep it around. Consumables are the secret weapon of gift-giving.

How early should I order a Father's Day gift?

At least 2 weeks before Father's Day is a safe window for standard shipping. Personalized or custom items often need 3 to 4 weeks. Digital gifts and gift cards can be sent same-day if you're cutting it close.
